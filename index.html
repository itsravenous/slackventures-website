Slackventures

A world of adventure awaits inside your chat app!

How it works

Obsessive DMs

Our DMs take their craft very seriously, paying particular attention to world building - cultures, languages and geography.

Immersive gameplay

Bespoke tools allow you to check your inventory, read maps and share lore, all from within Slack.

Weave a story together

It's not just about winning - the journey is important too. Upon completion of each Slackventure, players receive a lovingly hand-written novelisation of the action as a memento (PDF format).
