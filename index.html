How it works

Obsessive DMs

Our DMs take their craft very seriously, paying particular attention to:

  • World building - cultures, languages and geographies
  • Responsiveness - chances are, if you're online, your DM is too, or will be soon

Unique game mechanics

Slackventures were born out of RPG-loving friends who lacked the free time to commit to a dedicated questing session each week. The mechanics of Slackventures mean you can:

  • Play whenever you like
  • Take a break without holding up the rest of the group
  • Party up or play solo
  • Carve your own destiny - linear story arc optional!

Weave a story together

It's not just about winning - the journey is important too. Slackventures:

  • Encourage rich, collaborative storytelling
  • Feature published writers as players and DMs
  • Include a free PDF novelisation once the adventure is finished
